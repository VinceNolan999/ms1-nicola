Hello!

Ever since I was a young child I have been passionate about singing and music. I feel like I come alive when performing.  I started writing my own songs as a child and love expressing myself through music, which is why now I am studying music production and songwriting at University. I’ve been an avid performer for as long as I can remember. I attended stage school, performing in west end theatres, one of which being the Royal Albert Hall where I performed a solo. I have performed as part of an ensemble at local theatres. I also starred in the west end show 'Joseph and the amazing technicolored dream coat'. These were all fantastic opportunities that gained me a lot of experience.

After studying performing arts at college, I carried on performing whenever I could. I applied for auditions, went to open mic nights, had singing lessons and formed a Girl Band. I accepted any opportunity to perfom live. I enjoy volunteering to sing in places like local hospices and local festivals. Since Covid I have continued my musical journey by performing virtual gigs online so I can still fulfill my desire to perform.
